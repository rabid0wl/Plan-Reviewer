## Fees — Buena Park ADU Projects

### ADU Fee Exemptions

| ADU Size | Impact Fee Status |
|----------|------------------|
| **Less than 750 sq ft** | **Exempt** from impact fees and park land dedication fees (per Gov. Code § 66311.5) |
| **750 sq ft or more** | Proportionate park fees based on primary dwelling square footage |

Impact fees for ADUs are capped at **50% of those for the main house** per state law.

### Development Impact Fees (Ch. 3.60)

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| **Traffic Impact — GMA #1** (north of I-5) | $207 per PM peak hour trip generated |
| **Traffic Impact — GMA #2** (south of I-5) | $109 per PM peak hour trip generated |
| **Residential Police Impact Fee** | $12,500 per unit |
| **Park Land Dedication / In-Lieu** | Per Ch. 18.64 — based on 3 acres per 1,000 persons standard |

**ecode360 URL**: https://ecode360.com/42915704 (Revenue and Finance)

### Building Permit Fees

- Based on project valuation using **ICC Building Valuation Data (February 2023)**
- **4% surcharge** on all Building fees (exception: repairs/alterations to SFR and commercial/industrial TI with no expansion of square footage)
- Fees set by resolution (Resolution No. 14482), not codified in municipal code

### School Impact Fees

- Applicable if ADU exceeds **499 sq ft**
- Contact local school district(s) for current rates

### Park Land Dedication and In-Lieu Fees (ADU-specific)

Per § 19.348.010(C.15) and Chapter 18.64:
- **ADUs under 750 sq ft**: Exempt
- **ADUs 750 sq ft or more**: Proportionate amount based on square footage of primary dwelling
- **ADUs on multifamily properties**: Calculated based on proportion of total units in the dwelling

### Estimated Fee Categories for an ADU Project

1. Building permit fee (valuation-based + 4% surcharge)
2. Plan check fee (~65% of permit fee, typical)
3. OCFA fire review fee (if OCFA review required)
4. School impact fee (if ADU > 499 sq ft)
5. Park development in-lieu fee (if ADU >= 750 sq ft)
6. Sewer/water connection fees (if new connection needed)
7. Electrical/mechanical/plumbing permit fees
8. Orange County Sanitation District fees (possible)
9. Elevation permit fee (from City Engineer)
