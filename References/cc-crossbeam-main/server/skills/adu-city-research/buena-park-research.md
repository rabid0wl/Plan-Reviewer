## 3. Local Building Code Amendments

### 3A. CBC Amendments (SS 15.56.020)

The city has adopted the 2022 CBC with local amendments, additions, deletions, and exceptions. The 2022 CBC Amendments are included as an attachment to Title 15.

Known local amendments include:
- **Modifications to definitions** (local usage)
- **Address identification requirements** (address numbers, visibility)
- **Sprinkler and standpipe systems** — design of hydraulically calculated fire sprinkler systems shall not exceed 90% of water supply capacity (exception: when static pressure exceeds 100 psi)
- **Automatic fire-extinguishing systems** — required in all occupancies when total building area exceeds 5,000 sq ft or is more than two stories in height

**URL for amendments section:** [qcode.us/codes/buenapark - 15.56.020](https://qcode.us/codes/buenapark/view.php?frames=on&topic=15-15_56-15_56_020)

## 4. Grading and Excavation Code (Ch. 15.86)

**URL:** [ecode360.com/42924164](https://ecode360.com/42924164)

### Adoption

The city has adopted the **1993 Orange County Grading and Excavation Code** by reference as its grading and excavation code.

### Key Requirements

- **Stormwater/Construction Activity:** No grading permit for construction activities disturbing 5 acres or more (or less than 5 acres if part of a larger common plan of development 5+ acres) until proof of coverage with the State General Permit for construction activity is verified.
- Specific drainage slope requirements, excavation rules, and permit thresholds are governed by the referenced Orange County Grading and Excavation Code rather than local code provisions.

### Elevation Permits (Ch. 15.20)

Any person constructing or erecting a building or structure for which a building permit is required must **first obtain an elevation permit** from the City Engineer.

## 6. ADU Ordinance (SS 19.348.010)

### Location Confirmed

**Section:** SS 19.348.010 — Accessory Dwelling Units and Junior Accessory Dwelling Units
**Chapter:** 19.348 — Special Requirements for Certain Uses
**ecode360 URL:** [ecode360.com/47269780](https://ecode360.com/47269780) (also [42911497](https://ecode360.com/42911497))
**QCode URL:** [qcode.us - 19.348.010](https://library.qcode.us/lib/buena_park_ca/pub/city_code/item/title_19-division_3-chapter_19_348-19_348_010)

### Purpose

To establish regulations for ADUs and JADUs in compliance with California Government Code Sections 65852.2 and 65852.22, and to provide local standards to increase the supply of smaller and affordable housing units while ensuring compatibility with existing neighborhoods.

### Allowed Locations

- Lots zoned **single-family residential** containing or proposed to contain a single-family dwelling
- Lots zoned **multifamily residential or mixed-use residential** containing or proposed to contain a multifamily dwelling

### ADU Types

- **Attached** to primary dwelling
- **Detached** (standalone structure)
- **Conversion** of existing primary residence space (including attached garage)
- **Conversion** of existing accessory structure
- **Garage conversion** (no replacement parking required)
- **Attic/basement conversion** (with independent entrance, kitchen, bathroom)

### Size Limits

| Type | Maximum Floor Area |
|------|-------------------|
| **Detached ADU** | 1,200 sq ft |
| **Attached ADU** | 50% of primary dwelling OR 1,200 sq ft (whichever is less) |
| **JADU** | 500 sq ft |

### Height Limits

| Condition | Maximum Height |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Detached ADU** (standard) | 16 feet |
| **Attached ADU** (on single-family lot) | 2 stories / 30 feet |
| **Detached ADU** within 1/2 mile of major transit stop or high-quality transit corridor | 20 feet |
| **Above-garage units** | 18-20 feet (depending on setback distances) |

### Setback Requirements

| Condition | Setback |
|-----------|---------|
| **Converted existing structure** (same location/dimensions) | Maintain existing setbacks |
| **New construction ADU** | **4-foot minimum** (side and rear) |

### Parking Requirements

| Condition | Spaces Required |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Standard** | 1 space per ADU (max 1 per bedroom) |
| **Near transit** (within 1/2 mile walking distance of public transit) | **0 spaces** |
| **Historic district** | **0 spaces** |
| **Garage conversion** | **0 replacement spaces** |
| **Other qualifying exemptions per state law** | **0 spaces** |

### Units Allowed Per Lot

| Lot Type | Maximum |
|----------|---------|
| Single-family lot | 1 ADU + 1 JADU |
| Multifamily lot | Per state law provisions |

### Owner Occupancy

- **ADU:** No owner-occupancy requirement
- **JADU:** Owner must occupy either the main house or the JADU

### Fire Safety

- Fire sprinklers **NOT required** for ADU/JADU if not required for the primary residence or multifamily dwelling
- Construction of an ADU shall NOT trigger requirements to install fire sprinklers in any existing main dwelling
- Must comply with all current State and local building and fire codes

### Permit Processing

- **Ministerial approval** (no discretionary review, no public hearing)
- **60-day processing timeline** from receipt of complete application

### Impact Fees

- ADUs **less than 750 sq ft**: **exempt from impact fees**
- ADUs **750 sq ft or more**: proportionate Park Land Dedication and In-Lieu Fees based on square footage of primary dwelling (computed per Chapter 18.64)

### Design Standards

- Design review and architectural standards apply
- Fire department access requirements apply
- Utility connection fees may apply

## 7. Special Requirements

### 7A. Stormwater Drainage

**Chapter reference:** [ecode360.com/42923488](https://ecode360.com/42923488)

- Authorized inspector may inspect property for compliance with stormwater drainage regulations
- Discharge points, drainage patterns evaluated for compliance with permits issued relating to discharge to the stormwater drainage system

### 7B. Flood Hazard / Flood Damage Prevention

- Flood hazard areas of Buena Park are subject to periodic inundation
- Purpose: minimize public and private losses due to flood conditions
- Uses vulnerable to floods must be protected against flood damage at time of initial construction
- **Flood Plain/Sea Level Rise Overlay District** standards: prevent flood damage by elevating occupiable space, flood-proofing areas beneath flood elevations
- **Stormwater retention requirement:** 100% of drainage from impervious surfaces must be captured and retained on site with sufficient storage to keep the **first 1.85 inches of rainwater** from an individual rain event on site without discharging onto neighboring properties or rights-of-way (unless regional stormwater management system available)

### 7C. Fire Safety (Title 16)

**URL:** [ecode360.com/42924735](https://ecode360.com/42924735)

- 2022 California Fire Code adopted (IFC 2021 Edition)
- **Automatic fire-extinguishing system required** in all occupancies when total building area exceeds 5,000 sq ft or more than 2 stories
- Sprinkler system design: shall not exceed 90% of water supply capacity
- ADU-specific: fire sprinklers not required if not required for primary residence

### 7D. Utility Connections

**Underground Utility Districts:** [ecode360.com/42923099](https://ecode360.com/42923099)
- City has underground utility district provisions
- **Utilities and Mechanical Equipment standards:** [ecode360.com/42911420](https://ecode360.com/42911420)

### 7E. Elevation Permits (Ch. 15.20)

- Required before construction of any building/structure that requires a building permit
- Must be obtained from the City Engineer
- Establishes finished floor elevations, drainage patterns
