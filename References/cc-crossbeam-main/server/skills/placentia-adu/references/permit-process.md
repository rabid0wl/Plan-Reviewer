## Permit Process — Placentia

### Application Process Overview

1. Determine property eligibility (zoning, lot size, existing structures)
2. Define needs and budget
3. Evaluate lot compatibility
4. Design unit
5. Hire contractor
6. Submit to Planning Department with required documentation
7. Obtain Building Permit

### CRITICAL: Public Works BEFORE Building Plan Check

Placentia has a unique sequential requirement:
1. **Public Works submittal** (utility plan, grading permit docs, soils report) must be submitted PRIOR to or concurrently with building plans
2. **Planning Division** review occurs concurrently with Public Works
3. **Building plan check will NOT start** until approval letters are received from BOTH Planning and Public Works
4. Once Planning and PW approved, submit to Building Division for plan check and building permit (additional fees apply)

### Processing Timeline

- **60 days** maximum for ministerial review of a complete application
- Automatic approval if city fails to act within 60 days (per state law)
- No discretionary review, public hearing, or CEQA required

### Submittal Requirements

**Plans:**
- **3 sets** of full-size plans (24" × 36")
- **1 electronic copy** (PDF format)
- Drawn to engineering/architectural scale, fully dimensioned
- **Wet stamp and wet signature** by CA licensed Architect or Engineer

**Structural:**
- **2 sets** of structural calculations (wet stamp, wet signature by CA licensed Engineer)

**Energy:**
- **2 sets** of Title 24 energy calculations

### Required Plan Sheets (in order)

**1. Site Plan:**
- Title block (project name, location, applicant, architect/engineer contact)
- North arrow, scale
- Vicinity map with major streets
- Legal property lines and lot dimensions (per OC Assessor's Parcel Map)
- Required street dedications and improvements
- All proposed and existing structures (including on adjacent properties)
- Setback dimensions to all property lines
- Walls, fences, gates (location, height, material, color)
- Above-ground equipment (utility poles, transformers, A/C units)
- Easements and dimensions
- Access points, drive aisles, parking spaces, loading spaces, RV spaces
- Exterior lighting fixtures
- **Site summary table:** gross/net acreage, parking required/provided, lot coverage permitted/proposed, landscaped area required/provided, gross floor area

**2. Grading and Drainage Plan:**
- Existing and proposed contours on site and within **100 feet** of boundaries
- Proposed elevation
- All structures and improvements (curbs, gutters, driveways, sidewalks, median islands) on site and within 100 feet

**3. Landscape Plan:**
- Plant palette (names, types, sizes)
- Irrigation system (type, water mains, control valves, hose bibs)
- Must comply with **Chapter 23.77 Xeriscape Ordinance**

**4. Building Elevations:**
- All buildings, structures, wall-mounted signs, exterior lights, solar panels
- Exterior finishes and colors noted
- **Color chips and material samples** mounted on 8½" × 14" display board
- Cross section of structures

**5. Floor Plans:**
- Complete floor plans of existing and proposed conditions
- Room dimensions, floor area calculations, labeled room uses
- Door, closet, wall, cooking facility details

### Building Permit Additional Requirements

To pull a building permit:
- Valid government-issued ID
- State Contractor's ID card with workers' comp insurance verification
- City business license number
- Notarized authorization letter if agent acting for contractor/owner
- Grant deed (may be required)

### Fees Due at Plan Check

- Building plan check fees
- Public Works fees (if applicable)
- Library fees (if ADU ≥ 750 sq ft)
- School district fees (if ADU > 500 sq ft) — $3.48/sq ft to PYLUSD
- Development impact fees (if ADU ≥ 750 sq ft)

### Inspection Process

- **Schedule:** Monday-Thursday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- Provide project address, permit number, inspection type, contact info, gate code
- **No same-day inspections**
- **24-hour cancellation notice** required
- **$119 re-inspection fee** for same-day cancellation, no-show, or unready project
- Call after 8:30 AM on inspection day for 2-hour window estimate
