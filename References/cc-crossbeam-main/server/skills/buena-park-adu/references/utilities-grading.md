## Utilities and Grading — Buena Park

### Water Service

- **Provider**: City of Buena Park (municipal water) + Metropolitan Water District (wholesale)
- Suburban Water Systems (SouthWest Water Company) also provides service in portions of Buena Park
- **Contact**: 714-562-3721
- **URL**: https://www.buenapark.com/city_departments/public_works/utilities/water/index.php

### Sewer Service

- **Provider**: City of Buena Park Sewer Services Division (Public Works Department)
- Maintains 168+ miles of sanitary sewer mains and 3,400+ manholes
- **Contact**: 714-562-3721
- **URL**: https://www.buenapark.com/city_departments/public_works/utilities/sewer_services/index.php

### ADU Utility Connection Notes

- ADU ordinance allows metering with primary dwelling (per Gov. Code § 66324)
- ADUs sharing sewer/water laterals with primary dwelling may not need new connection (depends on city policy)
- ADUs under 750 sq ft are exempt from impact fees but **NOT** from capacity/connection fees (sewer connection fees are not "impact fees" per state statute)
- Specific connection fee amounts not published online — contact Public Works at 714-562-3721

### Electrical Service

- **Provider**: Southern California Edison (SCE)
- Panel upgrade may be required for ADU
- SCE service upgrade information available on city Building Division handouts page

### Underground Utility Districts

- City has underground utility district provisions
- **ecode360 URL**: https://ecode360.com/42923099

### Grading and Excavation Code (Ch. 15.86)

- **Adopted code**: 1993 Orange County Grading and Excavation Code (by reference)
- **Reference**: Orange County Grading Manual (OC Public Works, 2017 Edition)
- **ecode360 URL**: https://ecode360.com/42924164

**Key requirements**:
- **Stormwater/Construction Activity**: No grading permit for construction activities disturbing 5+ acres (or less than 5 acres if part of a larger common plan of 5+ acres) until proof of coverage with State General Permit for construction activity is verified
- Specific drainage slope requirements and permit thresholds governed by the OC Grading Code

### Elevation Permits (Ch. 15.20)

Required from City Engineer **before** any building construction. Establishes:
- Finished floor elevations
- Drainage patterns

**URL**: https://ecode360.com/42923927

### Stormwater Drainage

- **ecode360 URL**: https://ecode360.com/42923488
- Authorized inspector may inspect property for compliance with stormwater drainage regulations
- Discharge points and drainage patterns evaluated for compliance with discharge permits

### Flood Damage Prevention / Flood Overlay District

- Buena Park has flood hazard areas subject to periodic inundation (FEMA flood maps apply)
- **Flood Plain/Sea Level Rise Overlay District** exists
- Standards: prevent flood damage by elevating occupiable space, flood-proofing areas beneath flood elevations

**Stormwater retention requirement**: 100% of drainage from impervious surfaces must be captured and retained on site with sufficient storage to keep the **first 1.85 inches of rainwater** from an individual rain event on site without discharging onto neighboring properties or rights-of-way (unless regional stormwater management system available).
